Marvel Comics, formerly known as Marvel Publishing Company and Marvel Comics Group, is an American comics company owned by Marvel Entertainment. Since 2008, Marvel Studios, a subsidiary of Marvel Entertainment, has begun filming the Marvel Cinematic Universe series, bringing the stories from the comics to the screen again; in 2009, Marvel Studios became a subsidiary of the Walt Disney Pictures Group.

STAN LEE

Stan Lee, whose real name is Stanley Martin Lieber, is a Jewish-American comics editor, actor, producer, publisher, television personality, and former head and chairman of Marvel Comics. Stan Lee has created Spider-Man, Marvel 4 Superman, X-Men, Avengers, Iron Man, Hulk, Thor, Daredevil, Strange with artists such as Jack Kirby and Steve Ditko Doctor and other well-known comic characters.

KALVIN FEIGE

Kevin Feige, PGA is an American male executive producer and president of Marvel Studios. His films have grossed over $14 billion worldwide. And he is the most successful filmmaker in history. In April 2015, it was announced that his contract with Marvel Studios would expire in 2018. In October 2019, he became the Chief Creative Director of Marvel Entertainment.
